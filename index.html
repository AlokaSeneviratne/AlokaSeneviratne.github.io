About

I like working close to the hardware. My background runs from engineering physics through embedded firmware, IoT system design and signal processing, with side excursions into FPGA design and technical teaching.

Recent work includes a Master's thesis on synthetic IMU time-series generation for Freezing of Gait detection, a BLE mesh sensor architecture for environmental monitoring, and breath cycle detection from respiratory audio in MATLAB.

Before moving into embedded engineering I worked in HR software consulting and spent several years lecturing mathematics, which left me with a habit of explaining things clearly.
